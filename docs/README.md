## ğŸ¤ What is Voice Input?

Voice Input allows you to create forms by speaking naturally instead of manually adding fields. Simply describe what you need, and our AI will generate the form for you.

### Key Features

- âœ… **Natural Speech**: Speak naturally, no special commands needed
- âœ… **Real-Time Transcription**: See your words appear as you speak
- âœ… **Multi-Language**: Support for 6 major languages
- âœ… **Privacy-First**: Speech processed locally in your browser
- âœ… **Editable**: Edit transcriptions before generating forms
- âœ… **Smart AI**: Understands context and creates appropriate fields

## ğŸš€ Quick Start

### 1. Enable Voice Input

Click the **microphone button** in the Form Builder header.

### 2. Grant Permission

Allow microphone access when your browser asks.

### 3. Start Speaking

Describe your form naturally:

> "I need a contact form with name, email, phone, and message. Name and email are required."

### 4. Generate Form

Click **"Generate Form"** and watch your form appear!

### Contact Forms

**Description**:
> "I need a contact form with full name, email address, phone number, and a message box. Name and email are required."

**Result**: 4 fields (text, email, text, textarea) with validation

---

### Registration Forms

**Description**:
> "Create a registration form with first name, last name, email, password, confirm password, and a checkbox to agree to terms. All fields are required. Password must be at least 8 characters."

**Result**: 6 fields with password validation and terms checkbox

---

### Survey Forms

**Description**:
> "I need a customer satisfaction survey with a rating from 1 to 5, multiple choice for favorite product with options laptop, phone, and tablet, and a comment box for additional feedback."

**Result**: Rating field, radio buttons, and textarea

---

### Event Registration

**Description**:
> "Create an event registration form with attendee name, email, company name, job title, dietary restrictions as a dropdown with none, vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free, and a checkbox for bringing a guest."

**Result**: Complete event registration form with dropdown and checkbox
